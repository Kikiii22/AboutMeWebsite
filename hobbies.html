MY HOBBIES

My favourite hobby is acting.I must say that acting is not my hobby, but it is my life.I was part of a theater group in Tetovo-Letecka Teatarska Trupa actively for 12 years,but now sadly because I am not living in Tetovo I am pausing with acting. I have been an actor for 12 years, played roles in 20 theatre plays and 120 reruns and I have had the lead roles in 7 plays. I was nominated for Best Supporting Actor on a festival in Kocani-DAF.

I love playing sports.I am playing mostly volleyball.I have been a professional Volleyball player (position middle blocker) in volleyball team ,,Top Sport“ in Tetovo. Been playing volleyball professionally for 10 years till high school. Learned to be a team player and leader of a team. Attended tournaments and won many trophies.

The most important thing that I am doing is Volunteering at Red Cross Organisation in Tetovo, North Macedonia. I am really happy that with my little help I can make someone’s day better. I have Organized a charity for donating gifts to kids from vulnerable families .Also, I have organized humanitarian event for kids with disabilities.
